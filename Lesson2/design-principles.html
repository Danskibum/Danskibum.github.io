White Space

https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org

The reason I choose the Churches website to illustrate white space is because the name of the church is not cluttered with other parts of the site and I like how they have white space between the images

Contrast

https://www.pacificoffice.com/

I Used Pacific Office Automation to illustrate Contrast because I like the use of white text on the blue background. The text is easy to read and stands out from the background.

Proximity

https://www.toyota.com/

I feel that the Toyota website display the principle of proximity. As you can see they have three distinct areas. The information that is about the same things are grouped together and are in the same proximity to each other.
