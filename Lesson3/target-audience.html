Target audience

This site is aimed at anyone that like being outdoor and enjoying nature. It does not matter your age or what gender you are. If you want to know what the weather is doing so you know what to take with you while you enjoy the outdoors.

Personas

Persona 1

David is a 65 year old male who like to garden.
He does not like to be in the rain so he looks to this
to see if he will be able to go out and garden today.

Persona 2

Abigail is a 25 year old female. She likes
to go out camping and hiking. She uses this site to plan
her trips. She does not mind getting wet but does not like
the cold so she want to make sure she will have a fun outing.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

David is wanting to go out and plan some new
flowers in his garden this weekend but he does not know if
it is going to rain. He goes to his favorite site to check
the weather.

Scenario 2

Abigail is looking to go on a hike this weekend
and she is hoping to have good weather so her friends will
want to come along with her. So, she goes and checks to see
what the temperature will be.

Scenario 3

Chad is a leader of a bunch of new scouts and
they are getting ready to go on there first camping trip.
He does not want them to have a bad time on there fist trip
so he checks the weather to make sure the scouts are prepared
for the weather that they will face.

Scenario 4

Paul is going on a weeks long hiking trip and he
does not want to get out on the trial to find out that he does
not have the proper gear for the weather. So, he goes and checks
the extended forecast.

Scenario 5

Linda has all here kids at home doing school but
she wants them to go out and play. She would like to get them
together with other kids so they can have more fun. She checks
he weather to pick a day with good weather so, she can schedule
a time with the other kids mother.
